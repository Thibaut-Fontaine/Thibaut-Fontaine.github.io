Éternel Retour

Embark on a transcendental journey with "Éternel Retour," where pulsating basslines and haunting melodies embody the spirit of rebellion and liberation associated with Nietzsche's Übermensch. Breaking free from societal constraints and embracing personal evolution, each track urges listeners to question the foundations of power and envision a future free from oppression. Drawing inspiration from psychedelic journeys, this album invites exploration of altered states of consciousness and expands perception, fusing Kraftwerk's futuristic sensibilities with the energy of EBM.

Embrace the transformative power of chaos as the concept of Neguentropia reverses entropy, creating order from chaos, reflecting the boundless potential of the human spirit amidst a collapsing state. The album "Éternel Retour" continues the rebellion and liberation, questioning power structures and envisioning a future beyond oppression, exploring the transformative power of chaos and the concept of Neguentropia.

With utmost respect for the electronic music legacy, a Kraftwerk remix offers a fresh perspective on their timeless melodies, merging their pioneering work with modern production techniques. Embrace the power of music to shape a world free from the shackles of the state and ignite a revolution within.
